## Healthcare AI

### **Medical Diagnosis Systems**
- **Challenge:** AI must be explainable for FDA SaMD compliance
- **Solution:** Complete provenance tracing - doctors can see exactly why AI recommended a diagnosis
- **Impact:** Full audit trails for regulatory reporting, improved trust

### **Medical Imaging**
- **Challenge:** Bias in radiology AI systems
- **Solution:** Real-time bias detection and adaptive mitigation
- **Impact:** Fairness across patient demographics, regulatory compliance

### **Drug Discovery**
- **Challenge:** Explainable AI for regulatory approval
- **Solution:** Multimodal validation across molecular structures and literature
- **Impact:** Faster regulatory approval, better scientific understanding
